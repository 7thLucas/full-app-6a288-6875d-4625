# ASCEND Career Pathway — Product Overview

## What It Is
A mobile-first career transition application. ASCEND Career Pathway gives professionals navigating career transitions a structured, step-by-step system to move from confusion and scattered action to a clear career transition plan with milestones, weekly priorities, and accountability.

## The Problem
Professionals facing career uncertainty lack a structured system to navigate transitions. Without clear milestones, weekly priorities, and accountability mechanisms, they default to scattered action — researching aimlessly, losing momentum, and never executing with confidence.

## The Solution
ASCEND Career Pathway provides a personalized portal that turns career transition ambiguity into a concrete, trackable journey. The entry point is a personalized dashboard showing the client's current goal, 7-month roadmap progress, this week's priorities, and the very next action step — so they always know exactly where they stand and what to do next.

## Target Audience
- Professionals navigating job transitions, pivots, or career re-entry
- Individuals who need structure, clarity, and execution support to land their next role or pivot successfully
- Career coaches who deliver structured transformation programs to clients

## Core Modules
- **Dashboard** — current goal, progress indicator, weekly priorities, next action step; always shows where the user stands and what to do next
- **Career Assessment** — skills inventory, interests exploration, experience review, and career direction recommendations
- **7-Month Roadmap** — phased career transition plan with milestones, action steps, and progress tracking
- **Resources** — resume guidance, interview preparation, and career development resources
- **AI Strategist** — career coaching chat, prioritization assistance, and action planning on demand

## Strategic Principles
- Clarity before complexity: always show the user where they are and what's next
- Execution over motivation: structured, tactical, and forward-moving — not inspirational
- The 7-month roadmap is the backbone — every module ties back to it
- Mobile-first accessibility: designed for busy professionals on the go
