# ASCEND Career Pathway — Product Overview

## What It Is
A client portal for career coaching professionals and their clients. ASCEND Career Pathway gives professionals navigating career transitions a structured, step-by-step system to move from confusion and scattered action to a clear career transition plan with milestones, weekly priorities, and accountability.

## The Problem
Professionals facing career uncertainty lack a structured system to navigate transitions. Without clear milestones, weekly priorities, and accountability mechanisms, they default to scattered action — researching aimlessly, losing momentum, and never executing with confidence.

## The Solution
ASCEND Career Pathway provides a personalized, coach-supported portal that turns career transition ambiguity into a concrete, trackable journey. The entry point is a personalized dashboard showing the client's current goal, 7-month roadmap progress, this week's priorities, and the very next action step — so they always know exactly where they stand and what to do next.

## Target Audience
- Career coaching clients navigating job transitions, pivots, or re-entry
- Professionals who need structure, clarity, and execution support to land their next role or pivot successfully
- Career coaches who deliver structured transformation programs to clients

## Brand
- **Product name:** ASCEND Career Pathway
- **Provider brand:** ASCEND Strategy Co
- **Tone:** Calm, confident, structured, results-oriented
- **Color palette:** Deep blue (#2563eb) primary, violet (#7c3aed) accent

## Core Features
- **Personalized dashboard** — current goal, 7-month roadmap progress, this week's priorities, next action step
- **Client intake form** — captures career history, aspirations, and constraints
- **Career assessment** — surfaces strengths, gaps, and transition readiness
- **7-month roadmap** — phased milestones with resource links
- **Weekly action plans** — specific, prioritized next steps every week
- **Progress tracking** — visual accountability against the roadmap
- **Resource library** — curated tools and content by phase
- **AI strategist chat** — on-demand guidance for challenges and opportunities
- **Mobile-first design** — accessible anywhere, anytime

## Strategic Principles
- Clarity before complexity: always show the client where they are and what's next
- Accountability built in: weekly check-ins and progress visibility are non-negotiable
- Coach-client alignment: the portal serves both the client's experience and the coach's delivery workflow
- The 7-month roadmap is the backbone — every feature ties back to it
